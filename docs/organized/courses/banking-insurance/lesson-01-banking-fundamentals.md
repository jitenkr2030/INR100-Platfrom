## What is Banking?

Banking is the business of accepting deposits and lending money. Banks act as financial intermediaries between people who have excess money (depositors) and those who need money (borrowers).

### Why Banks Matter for Personal Finance
- **Safe storage**: Banks provide secure places to keep your money
- **Easy access**: Access your money when you need it
- **Interest earnings**: Grow your money through interest
- **Payment services**: Transfer money and make payments
- **Credit access**: Borrow money when needed
- **Financial planning**: Help achieve your financial goals

## Types of Bank Accounts

### 1. **Savings Account**
**Purpose**: Safe storage and regular access to money

#### Features
- **Interest earning**: 3-6% annual interest
- **Liquidity**: Easy access anytime
- **Transactions**: Limited (usually 3-4 free transactions per month)
- **Minimum balance**: Varies by bank (₹1,000-₹10,000)
- **FD/PPF integration**: Can transfer to fixed deposits

#### Best For
- Emergency fund storage
- Regular savings
- Short-term goals (6 months to 2 years)
- Money you need to access frequently

#### Popular Banks
- **HDFC Bank**: 4.00% interest, ₹10,000 minimum balance
- **ICICI Bank**: 3.50% interest, ₹5,000 minimum balance
- **SBI**: 3.50% interest, ₹1,000 minimum balance
- **Axis Bank**: 4.00% interest, ₹10,000 minimum balance

### 2. **Current Account**
**Purpose**: Business and frequent transactions

#### Features
- **No interest**: Money doesn't earn interest
- **Unlimited transactions**: No limit on transactions
- **Overdraft facility**: Can withdraw more than balance
- **Cheque book**: Professional payments
- **High minimum balance**: ₹25,000-₹50,000

#### Best For
- Business owners
- Frequent transactions
- Professional payments
- Companies and firms

#### Popular Banks
- **HDFC Bank Current Account**: ₹50,000 minimum balance
- **ICICI Bank Current Account**: ₹25,000 minimum balance
- **SBI Current Account**: ₹25,000 minimum balance

### 3. **Fixed Deposit (FD)**
**Purpose**: Safe investment for fixed returns

#### Features
- **Higher interest**: 6-8% annual interest
- **Fixed tenure**: 7 days to 10 years
- **Penalty for early withdrawal**: Usually 0.5-1%
- **Loan facility**: Can take loan against FD
- **Tax-saving options**: 5-year tax-saving FDs

#### Types
- **Regular FD**: Standard fixed deposit
- **Tax-saving FD**: 5-year lock-in, tax deduction under 80C
- **Senior citizen FD**: Higher interest rates
- **Cumulative FD**: Interest compounded quarterly
- **Non-cumulative FD**: Interest paid monthly/quarterly

#### Best For
- Conservative investors
- Money you won't need for 1+ years
- Building emergency funds (in chunks)
- Tax planning (5-year FDs)

### 4. **Recurring Deposit (RD)**
**Purpose**: Regular savings with fixed returns

#### Features
- **Regular deposits**: Fixed amount every month
- **Higher interest**: Usually 0.5-1% higher than savings account
- **Tenure**: 6 months to 10 years
- **Loan facility**: Can take loan against RD
- **Auto-debit**: Automatic transfer from savings

#### Best For
- Regular savers
- People with fixed monthly income
- Goal-based savings
- Disciplined investing

## Choosing the Right Bank

### 1. **Compare Key Features**

#### Interest Rates
- **Savings account**: 3.5-4.5% per annum
- **Fixed deposits**: 6-8% per annum
- **Loan rates**: Personal loans 10-18%, home loans 8-12%
- **Credit card**: Annual fees and interest rates

#### Minimum Balance Requirements
- **Savings account**: ₹1,000-₹25,000
- **Current account**: ₹25,000-₹50,000
- **Penalties**: Charges for non-maintenance

#### Transaction Charges
- **Cash transactions**: Free at home branch
- **Non-home branch**: ₹5-20 per transaction
- **Inter-bank transfers**: ₹5-50 per transaction
- **SMS charges**: ₹10-50 per month

### 2. **Evaluate Convenience**

#### Branch Network
- **Near your home/work**: Easy access
- **Operating hours**: 9:30 AM - 3:30 PM (usually)
- **Weekend banking**: Some banks open on Saturdays
- **Extended hours**: Some branches open till 6 PM

#### Digital Services
- **Mobile app quality**: User-friendly interface
- **Internet banking**: Feature-rich online platform
- **Customer support**: 24/7 helpline
- **Response time**: Quick resolution of issues

### 3. **Check Fees and Charges**

#### Account Maintenance
- **Monthly charges**: ₹100-₹500 per month
- **Non-maintenance penalty**: ₹200-₹500 per month
- **ATM charges**: Free at own ATMs, ₹20-25 at others
- **SMS alerts**: ₹10-50 per month

#### Loan Processing
- **Processing fees**: 0.5-2% of loan amount
- **Documentation charges**: ₹500-₹2,000
- **Legal charges**: ₹1,000-₹5,000
- **Valuation fees**: For property loans

## Banking Terminology You Should Know

### Account-Related Terms
- **Account Number**: Unique identifier for your account
- **Customer ID**: Your bank's customer identifier
- **IFSC Code**: Bank branch identification code
- **MICR Code**: Magnetic ink character recognition code
- **CIF Number**: Customer Information File number

### Transaction Terms
- **Debit**: Money going out of your account
- **Credit**: Money coming into your account
- **Balance**: Current amount in your account
- **Overdraft**: Withdrawing more than available balance
- **Reversal**: Returning a transaction

### Interest and Loan Terms
- **Principal**: Original loan amount
- **Interest**: Cost of borrowing money
- **EMI**: Equated Monthly Installment
- **Tenure**: Loan repayment period
- **Collateral**: Security for loan
- **Credit Score**: Your creditworthiness rating

## Banking Security Best Practices

### 1. **Protect Your Account Information**

#### Never Share
- **Account number**: Only with trusted parties
- **Password/PIN**: Never share with anyone
- **OTP**: Don't share one-time passwords
- **CVV**: Never share card security code
- **Cheque book**: Keep secure and unused

#### Safe Practices
- **Strong passwords**: Use complex, unique passwords
- **Regular updates**: Change passwords every 3 months
- **Secure devices**: Use trusted computers/phones
- **Log out properly**: Always log out after online banking
- **Monitor statements**: Check regularly for unauthorized transactions

### 2. **Verify Before Transacting**

#### Bank Communications
- **Official numbers**: Only call bank from official website
- **Email verification**: Check sender's email address
- **Website security**: Look for "https" and padlock icon
- **Suspicious calls**: Banks never ask for sensitive info over phone

#### Transaction Verification
- **Recipient details**: Double-check account numbers
- **Amount confirmation**: Verify before confirming
- **UPI verification**: Check UPI ID carefully
- **Cheque endorsement**: Sign only on the back when depositing

### 3. **Report Suspicious Activity**

#### Immediate Actions
- **Unauthorized transactions**: Report immediately to bank
- **Lost/stolen cards**: Block cards immediately
- **Phishing attempts**: Report to bank and cyber crime
- **Suspicious calls**: Don't provide information, report to bank

#### Contact Information
- **24/7 helpline**: Save bank customer care numbers
- **Email support**: Use official bank email addresses
- **Branch visit**: Visit branch for serious issues
- **Cyber crime**: Report to cybercrime.gov.in

## Digital Banking Revolution

### 1. **UPI (Unified Payments Interface)**

#### What is UPI
- **Instant payments**: Transfer money 24/7
- **Bank agnostic**: Works across all banks
- **Mobile-first**: Designed for smartphone usage
- **Simple interface**: Easy to use for everyone

#### Popular UPI Apps
- **Google Pay**: Widely used, good features
- **PhonePe**: Good for bill payments
- **Paytm**: Popular for merchants
- **Amazon Pay**: Integrated with Amazon
- **BHIM**: Government-backed, secure

#### UPI Benefits
- **Instant transfer**: Money reaches immediately
- **No charges**: Most transactions free
- **QR code payments**: Scan and pay at shops
- **Request money**: Ask for payments from others

### 2. **Digital Wallets**

#### Types of Wallets
- **Bank wallets**: ICICI Pockets, HDFC PayZapp
- **Platform wallets**: Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay
- **Telecom wallets**: Airtel Money, JioMoney
- **Merchant wallets**: Amazon Pay, Flipkart Pay

#### Wallet Features
- **Online payments**: Shopping, bill payments
- **Offline payments**: QR codes at shops
- **Money transfer**: Send/receive money
- **Investment**: Buy mutual funds, insurance
- **Rewards**: Cashback and offers

### 3. **Digital Banking Trends**

#### Emerging Technologies
- **AI chatbots**: 24/7 customer service
- **Biometric authentication**: Fingerprint, face recognition
- **Voice banking**: Banking through voice commands
- **Blockchain**: Secure, transparent transactions
- **Open banking**: Data sharing between banks

#### Future of Banking
- **Neo banks**: Digital-only banks
- **Embedded banking**: Banking in other apps
- **Personalized services**: AI-driven recommendations
- **Instant services**: Faster loan processing
- **Global accessibility**: Banking across borders
