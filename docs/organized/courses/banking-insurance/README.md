# Banking & Insurance Course

## Course Overview
Master the fundamentals of banking services and insurance products. Learn to choose the right bank accounts, understand different types of insurance, and build a strong financial safety net for you and your family.

## Course Details
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Lessons**: 8 lessons
- **Level**: Beginner
- **XP Reward**: 150 XP per lesson (1,200 XP total)
- **Importance**: HIGH
- **Prerequisites**: Personal Finance Basics (recommended)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, you will be able to:
- Choose the right bank account for your needs
- Understand different types of insurance coverage
- Compare insurance policies and make informed decisions
- Build a comprehensive financial safety net
- Protect yourself and family from financial risks

## Course Structure

### Lesson 1: Banking Fundamentals
Learn about different types of bank accounts, services, and how to choose the right bank.

### Lesson 2: Digital Banking & Mobile Apps
Master online banking, mobile apps, and digital payment systems.

### Lesson 3: Credit and Debit Cards
Understand how to use credit and debit cards safely and effectively.

### Lesson 4: Loans and Borrowing
Learn about different types of loans and how to borrow responsibly.

### Lesson 5: Introduction to Insurance
Understand the basics of insurance and risk management.

### Lesson 6: Life Insurance
Learn about term life, whole life, and ULIP insurance products.

### Lesson 7: Health Insurance
Master health insurance plans, coverage, and claims process.

### Lesson 8: Insurance Planning
Create a comprehensive insurance strategy for your family.

## Course Features
- Interactive calculators for insurance needs assessment
- Comparison tools for bank and insurance products
- Case studies and real-world examples
- Practical checklists and decision frameworks

## Completion Benefits
- Comprehensive banking and insurance knowledge
- Ability to make informed financial decisions
- Better protection against financial risks
- Foundation for advanced financial planning courses
