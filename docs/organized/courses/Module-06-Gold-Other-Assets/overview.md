# Module 06: Gold & Other Assets

## Overview
This module covers alternative investment opportunities beyond traditional stocks, bonds, and mutual funds. Students will learn about real estate investment, precious metals, commodities, and other alternative asset classes that can help diversify portfolios and provide inflation protection.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand real estate investment fundamentals and strategies
- Learn about property types, market analysis, and financing options
- Explore legal aspects and due diligence in property investments
- Understand Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs)
- Learn about property taxation and portfolio management strategies

## Key Takeaways
- Real estate provides diversification benefits and inflation protection
- Proper research and due diligence are crucial for property investment
- REITs offer a liquid alternative to direct property investment
- Property investment requires significant capital and ongoing management
- Tax implications should be considered in investment decisions
