## Key Concepts Covered

### Risk Fundamentals
- **Types of Risk**: Market, company-specific, inflation, liquidity
- **Risk-Return Relationship**: Higher risk, higher potential returns
- **Risk Tolerance**: Personal capacity and willingness
- **Risk Management**: Systematic approach to risk control

### Quantitative Measures
- **Standard Deviation**: Return variability measurement
- **Beta**: Market sensitivity analysis
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Peak-to-trough decline
- **Value at Risk**: Maximum expected loss estimation

### Protection Strategies
- **Diversification**: Risk spreading across assets
- **Stop-Loss**: Systematic loss limitation
- **Asset Allocation**: Risk-appropriate portfolio mix
- **Emergency Funds**: Liquidity for unexpected events

### Crisis Management
- **Emergency Planning**: Comprehensive crisis response
- **Exit Strategies**: Systematic investment exits
- **Recovery Planning**: Post-crisis wealth rebuilding
- **Professional Help**: When and how to seek assistance
