# Scam Awareness Course

## Course Overview
**VERY IMPORTANT** - Protect yourself from financial fraud and investment scams. This critical course teaches you to identify, avoid, and report various types of financial scams that target investors.

## Course Details
- **Duration**: 1-2 hours
- **Lessons**: 7 lessons
- **Level**: Beginner
- **XP Reward**: 100 XP per lesson (700 XP total)
- **Importance**: CRITICAL
- **Prerequisites**: None

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, you will be able to:
- Identify common stock market fraud schemes
- Recognize pump-and-dump manipulations
- Spot WhatsApp/Telegram tip scams
- Verify legitimate brokers and platforms
- Identify fake websites and phishing attempts
- Recognize Ponzi schemes and get-rich-quick promises
- Develop safe investing habits for beginners

## Course Structure

### Lesson 1: Recognizing Stock Market Fraud
Learn to identify red flags in stock market advice and tips.

### Lesson 2: Pump-and-Dump Schemes  
Understand how manipulators inflate stock prices and how to protect yourself.

### Lesson 3: WhatsApp/Telegram Tip Scams
Identify social media-based investment scams and fake tips.

### Lesson 4: How to Verify Brokers
Learn to check broker credentials and regulatory compliance.

### Lesson 5: Fake Websites & Phishing
Spot fraudulent investment websites and phishing attempts.

### Lesson 6: How to Identify Ponzi Schemes
Recognize Ponzi schemes and pyramid schemes that promise unrealistic returns.

### Lesson 7: Safe Investing Habits for Beginners
Develop a checklist of safe practices for new investors.

## Safety First Approach
This course emphasizes safety over profits. Remember: **"If it sounds too good to be true, it probably is."**

## Completion Certificate
Upon completing all 7 lessons and passing the final assessment, you will earn the "Safety First" achievement and certificate.
