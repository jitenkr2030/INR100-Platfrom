## Detailed Lesson Breakdown

### Lesson 1: Recognizing Stock Market Fraud
**Duration**: 15-20 minutes
**Key Topics**:
- Types of stock market fraud (insider trading, market manipulation)
- Red flags in investment advice and opportunities
- How fraudsters target retail investors
- Case studies of real fraud cases
- Protection strategies and verification methods

**Learning Objectives**:
- Understand common fraud patterns
- Identify warning signs in investment opportunities
- Develop critical thinking about investment claims
- Learn verification processes for financial advisors

### Lesson 2: Pump-and-Dump Schemes
**Duration**: 15-20 minutes
**Key Topics**:
- How pump-and-dump schemes work in three phases
- Modern tactics using social media and online platforms
- Warning signs and red flags to watch for
- Real examples and case studies
- Protection strategies for individual investors

**Learning Objectives**:
- Understand the mechanics of market manipulation
- Identify early warning signs of price manipulation
- Recognize social media-based promotion tactics
- Learn to protect against pump-and-dump schemes

### Lesson 3: WhatsApp/Telegram Tip Scams
**Duration**: 15-20 minutes
**Key Topics**:
- Common messaging app investment scam patterns
- How scammers use group dynamics and social proof
- Verification techniques for tip providers
- Safety measures for messaging app usage
- Real victim stories and lessons learned

**Learning Objectives**:
- Identify common WhatsApp and Telegram scam patterns
- Recognize fake tips and misleading financial advice
- Implement safety measures for messaging app usage
- Learn proper verification processes for investment advice

### Lesson 4: How to Verify Brokers
**Duration**: 20-25 minutes
**Key Topics**:
- Types of broker registration in India (SEBI, exchanges, AMFI)
- Step-by-step verification process for stock brokers
- Checking depository participant registration
- Identifying red flags of fake brokers
- Regulatory databases and verification tools

**Learning Objectives**:
- Understand the importance of using registered brokers
- Learn to verify broker credentials through official channels
- Check broker compliance with regulatory requirements
- Recognize fake brokers and unauthorized entities

### Lesson 5: Fake Websites & Phishing
**Duration**: 20-25 minutes
**Key Topics**:
- Types of fake investment websites and their tactics
- Identifying phishing attempts via email, SMS, and phone calls
- Website verification techniques and security measures
- Protecting personal and financial information online
- Responding to suspected phishing attacks

**Learning Objectives**:
- Identify fake investment websites designed to steal money
- Recognize phishing attempts targeting financial information
- Implement security measures to protect against online fraud
- Verify website authenticity before providing information

### Lesson 6: How to Identify Ponzi Schemes
**Duration**: 20-25 minutes
**Key Topics**:
- How Ponzi schemes operate and sustain themselves
- Famous Ponzi scheme examples and case studies
- Warning signs of fraudulent investment programs
- Types of modern Ponzi schemes (crypto, real estate, forex)
- Protection strategies and due diligence processes

**Learning Objectives**:
- Understand how Ponzi schemes operate and maintain illusions
- Identify early warning signs of fraudulent investment programs
- Recognize the difference between legitimate and Ponzi investments
- Protect yourself and others from pyramid-style frauds

### Lesson 7: Safe Investing Habits for Beginners
**Duration**: 20-25 minutes
**Key Topics**:
- Building a comprehensive safe investing framework
- Creating personal investment guidelines and rules
- Establishing disciplined investment decision-making
- Building a network of trusted financial advisors
- Long-term wealth building with safety focus

**Learning Objectives**:
- Develop a comprehensive safe investing checklist
- Create personal investment guidelines and rules
- Establish a disciplined approach to investment decision-making
- Build a network of trusted financial advisors and resources
