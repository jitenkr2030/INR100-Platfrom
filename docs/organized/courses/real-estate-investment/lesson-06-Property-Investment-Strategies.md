## Section 3: Rental Yield Optimization

### Understanding Rental Yield

**Rental Yield Definition**
Rental yield is the annual rental income expressed as a percentage of the property's market value or purchase price.

**Types of Rental Yield**
1. **Gross Rental Yield**
   - Total rental income ÷ Property value × 100
   - Basic yield calculation
   - Doesn't account for expenses
   - Initial screening tool

2. **Net Rental Yield**
   - (Rental income - Expenses) ÷ Property value × 100
   - More accurate profitability measure
   - Accounts for all costs
   - Investment decision tool

3. **Cash-on-Cash Return**
   - Annual cash flow ÷ Total cash invested × 100
   - Measures actual cash returns
   - Accounts for financing
   - Investor-focused metric

**Rental Yield Benchmarks (India 2025)**
- **Residential Apartments**: 3-6% gross yield
- **Commercial Offices**: 7-10% gross yield
- **Retail Spaces**: 6-9% gross yield
- **Warehouses**: 8-12% gross yield
- **Serviced Apartments**: 5-8% gross yield

## Section 4: Property Flipping and Trading

### Property Flipping Fundamentals

**Definition and Types**
Property flipping involves purchasing properties with the intention of quickly reselling them for profit, typically within 1-2 years.

**Types of Flipping**
1. **Buy, Renovate, Sell (BRRR)**
   - Purchase below market value
   - Renovate and improve
   - Sell at market value
   - Focus on value addition

2. **Quick Turnaround**
   - Minimal improvements
   - Market timing focus
   - Short holding period
   - Speculative approach

3. **Development Flipping**
   - Land acquisition and development
   - Project management
   - Joint development deals
   - Commercial projects

**Market Conditions for Flipping**

- **Capital Appreciation**: 200% over 10 years

- **Portfolio Performance**: Exceeded expectations

- Quality property selection
