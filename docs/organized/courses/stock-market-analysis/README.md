# Stock Market Analysis Course

## Course Overview
Master the art and science of stock market analysis with our comprehensive 12-lesson course. Learn both fundamental and technical analysis techniques to make informed investment decisions.

## Course Details
- **Total Lessons**: 12
- **Duration**: 8-10 hours
- **Difficulty Level**: Intermediate to Advanced
- **Prerequisites**: Stock Market Foundations course
- **XP Reward**: 2400 XP (200 XP per lesson)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, you will:
- Master fundamental analysis techniques
- Learn technical analysis and charting
- Understand financial statement analysis
- Develop portfolio evaluation skills
- Create investment research reports
- Implement risk assessment methods

## Course Structure

### Module 1: Fundamental Analysis (Lessons 1-4)
1. **Introduction to Fundamental Analysis** - Understanding the principles
2. **Financial Statement Analysis** - Reading balance sheets, income statements, cash flow
3. **Financial Ratios and Metrics** - Key performance indicators
4. **Valuation Methods** - DCF, P/E, P/B, and other valuation models

### Module 2: Technical Analysis (Lessons 5-8)
5. **Chart Patterns and Trends** - Reading candlestick and line charts
6. **Technical Indicators** - Moving averages, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands
7. **Support and Resistance Levels** - Identifying key price levels
8. **Volume Analysis** - Understanding trading volume patterns

### Module 3: Advanced Analysis (Lessons 9-12)
9. **Sector and Industry Analysis** - Comparative analysis techniques
10. **Economic Indicators and Market Cycles** - Macroeconomic factors
11. **Investment Research Report Writing** - Creating professional reports
12. **Portfolio Construction and Analysis** - Building balanced portfolios

## Assessment
- **Quiz Questions**: 120 total (10 per lesson)
- **Practical Assignments**: 6 portfolio analysis projects
- **Final Project**: Complete investment research report

## Prerequisites
- Completion of Stock Market Foundations course
- Basic understanding of financial markets
- Familiarity with financial statements

## Certificate
Upon successful completion, receive the "Stock Market Analysis Expert" certificate.
