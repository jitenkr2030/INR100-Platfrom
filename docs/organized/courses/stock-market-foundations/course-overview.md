# Stock Market Foundations - Course Overview

## Course Information

**Course Title**: Stock Market Foundations
**Difficulty Level**: Beginner
**Duration**: 2-3 hours
**Total Lessons**: 10
**XP Reward**: 150 XP + 15 XP per lesson
**Prerequisites**: None - This is an introductory course

## Course Description

This comprehensive beginner-friendly course provides a solid foundation in stock market basics. Learn how stock markets work, understand different types of investments, and develop the knowledge to make informed investment decisions. Perfect for complete beginners who want to start their investment journey with confidence.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:
- Understand the basic functioning of stock markets
- Differentiate between primary and secondary markets
- Evaluate IPOs and understand the listing process
- Interpret market indices like NIFTY 50 and SENSEX
- Recognize market cycles and their implications
- Understand dividend investing and its benefits
- Classify companies by market capitalization
- Identify blue-chip companies and their characteristics
- Assess stock liquidity and trading implications
- Read market depth and order book data

## Course Structure

### Lesson 1: How the Stock Market Works
- **Duration**: 15-20 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Stock market basics, trading process, Indian exchanges (NSE/BSE)
- **Learning Outcome**: Understanding of fundamental market concepts

### Lesson 2: Primary Market vs. Secondary Market
- **Duration**: 15-20 minutes
- **Key Topics**: IPO process, trading mechanics, order types
- **Learning Outcome**: Clear distinction between company funding and investor trading

### Lesson 3: What is an IPO?
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: IPO evaluation, application process, success factors
- **Learning Outcome**: Ability to evaluate and participate in IPOs

### Lesson 4: What are Market Indices? (NIFTY, Sensex)
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Index calculation, sectoral indices, investing strategies
- **Learning Outcome**: Understanding of market benchmarking

### Lesson 5: Market Cycles: Bull vs. Bear
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Market psychology, cycle identification, investment strategies
- **Learning Outcome**: Recognition of different market phases

### Lesson 6: Understanding Dividends
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Dividend types, yield analysis, dividend investing
- **Learning Outcome**: Knowledge of shareholder returns

### Lesson 7: Market Cap: Small-cap, Mid-cap, Large-cap
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Company classification, risk-return profiles, portfolio construction
- **Learning Outcome**: Understanding of different investment categories

### Lesson 8: What are Blue-chip Companies?
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Blue-chip characteristics, quality assessment, investment strategies
- **Learning Outcome**: Ability to identify quality companies

### Lesson 9: What is Liquidity?
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Liquidity measurement, trading implications, risk management
- **Learning Outcome**: Understanding of trading ease and market efficiency

### Lesson 10: What is Market Depth?
- **Duration**: 25-30 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Order book analysis, price prediction, professional trading concepts
- **Learning Outcome**: Advanced market analysis skills
