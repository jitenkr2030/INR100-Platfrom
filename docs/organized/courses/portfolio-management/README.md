# Portfolio Management Course

## Course Overview
Master the art and science of managing investment portfolios with our comprehensive 10-lesson course. Learn to build, monitor, and optimize portfolios for different investment objectives and risk profiles.

## Course Details
- **Total Lessons**: 10
- **Duration**: 7-8 hours
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Prerequisites**: Stock Market Foundations, Stock Market Analysis
- **XP Reward**: 2000 XP (200 XP per lesson)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, you will:
- Design optimal portfolios using modern portfolio theory
- Implement various portfolio management strategies
- Monitor and rebalance portfolios systematically
- Evaluate portfolio performance and risk
- Apply advanced portfolio optimization techniques
- Manage portfolios for different client objectives

## Course Structure

### Module 1: Portfolio Theory and Design (Lessons 1-3)
1. **Modern Portfolio Theory Foundations** - Efficient frontier and risk-return optimization
2. **Asset Allocation Strategies** - Strategic vs. tactical allocation approaches
3. **Portfolio Construction Framework** - Step-by-step portfolio building process

### Module 2: Portfolio Management Strategies (Lessons 4-6)
4. **Active vs. Passive Management** - Choosing the right approach
5. **Risk Management Techniques** - Hedging and protection strategies
6. **Alternative Investment Integration** - REITs, commodities, and alternatives

### Module 3: Portfolio Implementation (Lessons 7-8)
7. **Portfolio Rebalancing Systems** - Systematic rebalancing methods
8. **Performance Evaluation and Attribution** - Measuring and analyzing results

### Module 4: Advanced Topics (Lessons 9-10)
9. **ESG and Sustainable Investing** - Environmental, social, and governance factors
10. **Portfolio Optimization in Practice** - Real-world application and case studies

## Assessment
- **Quiz Questions**: 100 total (10 per lesson)
- **Practical Assignments**: 5 portfolio construction projects
- **Final Project**: Complete portfolio management plan

## Prerequisites
- Completion of Stock Market Foundations course
- Completion of Stock Market Analysis course
- Basic understanding of financial markets and investment principles

## Certificate
Upon successful completion, receive the "Professional Portfolio Manager" certificate.
