## High Net Worth Individual (HNWI) Overview

### Wealth Classification in India

#### Net Worth Categories
**Ultra High Net Worth (UHNW)**:
- **Definition**: Net worth above ₹500 crore
- **Characteristics**: Complex financial structures, global presence
- **Services**: Dedicated relationship managers, family offices
- **Investment**: Sophisticated strategies, alternative investments

**High Net Worth (HNW)**:
- **Definition**: Net worth ₹25 crore - ₹500 crore
- **Characteristics**: Significant investible wealth, business interests
- **Services**: Premium wealth management, private banking
- **Investment**: Diversified portfolios, alternative assets

**Mass Affluent**:
- **Definition**: Net worth ₹5 crore - ₹25 crore
- **Characteristics**: Growing wealth, professional success
- **Services**: Relationship-based wealth management
- **Investment**: Traditional and some alternative investments

#### HNWI Characteristics
**Demographics**:
- **Age**: Typically 40-60 years old
- **Education**: Highly educated professionals
- **Occupation**: Business owners, senior executives, professionals
- **Family Status**: Often married with children

**Financial Profile**:
- **Multiple Income Sources**: Business, investments, salary
- **Complex Assets**: Real estate, businesses, securities
- **Tax Complexity**: Multiple jurisdictions, complex structures
- **Liquidity Needs**: Varying liquidity requirements
