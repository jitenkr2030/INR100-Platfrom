# Financial Derivatives Course

## Course Overview
Master the complex world of financial derivatives including options, futures, swaps, and structured products with our comprehensive 8-lesson course designed for advanced investors.

## Course Details
- **Total Lessons**: 8
- **Duration**: 6-7 hours
- **Difficulty Level**: Advanced
- **Prerequisites**: Stock Market Foundations, Stock Market Analysis, Portfolio Management
- **XP Reward**: 1600 XP (200 XP per lesson)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, you will:
- Understand the fundamental principles of derivatives
- Master options trading strategies and risk management
- Learn futures markets and hedging techniques
- Comprehend swap structures and applications
- Evaluate derivative pricing and valuation methods
- Apply derivatives in portfolio management and risk hedging

## Course Structure

### Module 1: Derivatives Fundamentals (Lessons 1-2)
1. **Introduction to Derivatives** - Types, purposes, and market structure
2. **Options Fundamentals** - Call and put options, intrinsic and time value

### Module 2: Options Trading (Lessons 3-4)
3. **Options Strategies** - Covered calls, protective puts, spreads, straddles
4. **Advanced Options Concepts** - Greeks, volatility, and pricing models

### Module 3: Futures and Forwards (Lessons 5-6)
5. **Futures Markets** - Trading mechanisms, margin requirements, delivery
6. **Hedging with Futures** - Risk management and basis trading

### Module 4: Advanced Derivatives (Lessons 7-8)
7. **Swaps and Structured Products** - Interest rate swaps, credit derivatives
8. **Derivatives in Portfolio Management** - Risk management and optimization

## Assessment
- **Quiz Questions**: 80 total (10 per lesson)
- **Practical Assignments**: 4 derivatives trading simulations
- **Final Project**: Complete derivatives risk management plan

## Prerequisites
- Completion of Stock Market Foundations course
- Completion of Stock Market Analysis course
- Completion of Portfolio Management course
- Strong understanding of financial markets and risk management
