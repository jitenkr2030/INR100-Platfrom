# Module 1: Money Basics Course

## Course Overview

This foundational course introduces the fundamental concepts of money, its evolution, and its role in modern economics. Designed as the cornerstone of financial literacy, this module provides essential knowledge that forms the basis for all advanced financial concepts.

## Course Structure

### Target Audience
- Complete beginners to financial concepts
- Students and young professionals
- Anyone seeking to understand money fundamentals
- Prerequisites for advanced investment courses

### Learning Objectives
Upon completion of this module, students will:
- Understand what money is and how it evolved
- Comprehend different types of money and digital payments
- Learn about India's monetary system and RBI's role
- Master basic financial mathematics and concepts
- Develop financial awareness and planning skills

## Course Curriculum

### Lesson 01: What is Money? - Evolution and Definition
**Focus**: Understanding money's fundamental nature and historical development
- Definition of money and its functions
- Historical evolution from barter to digital currency
- Why money became essential for trade
- Different forms money has taken throughout history
- Money as a store of value, medium of exchange, and unit of account

### Lesson 02: Types of Money - Cash, Digital, and Cryptocurrency
**Focus**: Modern money systems and payment methods
- Physical cash and its limitations
- Digital money and electronic payments
- Bank deposits and how they work
- Cryptocurrency basics and blockchain technology
- Future of money and digital payments in India

### Lesson 03: How Money Works - The Monetary System
**Focus**: Understanding how money moves through the economy
- Money creation by banks
- How money supply affects the economy
- The role of central banks
- Money circulation and velocity
- Economic indicators related to money

### Lesson 04: Indian Currency System - Rupee and RBI
**Focus**: India's monetary framework and institutions
- Indian Rupee (₹) history and design
- Reserve Bank of India's role and functions
- Monetary policy tools and objectives
- Currency printing and security features
- Exchange rates and foreign exchange

### Lesson 05: Interest and Inflation - Core Economic Concepts
**Focus**: Fundamental economic principles affecting money
- What is interest and why it exists
- Types of interest rates and their applications
- Understanding inflation and its causes
- Real vs nominal returns
- Interest rate impact on savings and investments

### Lesson 06: Time Value of Money - Future and Present Value
**Focus**: Mathematical foundations of financial planning
- Why money has time value
- Future value calculations and applications
- Present value concepts and discounting
- Compounding and its power
- Practical applications in financial planning

### Lesson 07: Money vs Wealth - Fundamental Difference
**Focus**: Understanding true wealth creation concepts
- Difference between money and wealth
- Assets vs liabilities
- Income vs wealth building
- Financial independence principles
- Creating multiple income streams

### Lesson 08: Financial Goals - Setting and Planning
**Focus**: Goal-based financial planning
- Types of financial goals (short, medium, long-term)
- SMART goal setting methodology
- Goal prioritization and sequencing
- Milestone tracking and adjustments
- Creating financial action plans

### Lesson 09: Financial Mathematics - Basic Calculations
**Focus**: Essential math skills for financial decision-making
- Percentage calculations
- Simple vs compound interest calculations
- EMI calculations for loans
- Investment return calculations
- Tax calculations basics

### Lesson 10: Money Management - Budgeting and Savings
**Focus**: Practical money management skills
- Budgeting principles and methods
- 50-30-20 rule and other budgeting frameworks
- Emergency fund creation
- Savings strategies and automation
- Money tracking and expense management
