# Emergency Fund & Debt Management Course

## Course Overview
Master the fundamentals of building financial resilience through emergency fund creation and debt management. Learn to protect yourself from financial shocks and eliminate debt systematically for long-term financial freedom.

## Course Details
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Lessons**: 7 lessons
- **Level**: Beginner
- **XP Reward**: 150 XP per lesson (1,050 XP total)
- **Importance**: HIGH
- **Prerequisites**: Personal Finance Basics (recommended)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, you will be able to:
- Build a robust emergency fund for financial security
- Understand different types of debt and their impact
- Create systematic debt elimination strategies
- Balance emergency fund building with debt repayment
- Develop financial resilience and stress-free finances

## Course Structure

### Lesson 1: Emergency Fund Fundamentals
Learn the importance of emergency funds and how to build one effectively.

### Lesson 2: Calculating Your Emergency Fund Needs
Determine the right amount for your emergency fund based on your situation.

### Lesson 3: Building Your Emergency Fund
Strategies and methods to build your emergency fund systematically.

### Lesson 4: Understanding Debt Types and Impact
Learn about different types of debt and their financial impact.

### Lesson 5: Debt Assessment and Prioritization
Evaluate your debt and prioritize which debts to tackle first.

### Lesson 6: Debt Elimination Strategies
Master proven methods to eliminate debt systematically.

### Lesson 7: Balancing Emergency Fund and Debt
Learn to balance emergency fund building with debt repayment.

## Course Features
- Emergency fund calculators
- Debt tracking worksheets
- Debt elimination strategies
- Financial resilience planning tools

## Completion Benefits
- Complete financial security foundation
- Debt elimination roadmap
- Stress-free financial management
- Strong emergency preparedness
