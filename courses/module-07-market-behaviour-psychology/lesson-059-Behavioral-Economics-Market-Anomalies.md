#### **Prospect Theory**
**Loss Aversion**:
- **Asymmetric Valuation**: Losses hurt more than equivalent gains
- **Reference Dependence**: Decisions based on reference points

- **Status Quo Bias**: Preference for maintaining current state

**Mental Accounting**:
- **Separate Accounts**: Treating money differently based on source
- **Budget Constraints**: Self-imposed budget constraints
- **Sunk Cost**: Irreversible past investments affect decisions
- **Framing Effects**: Different decisions based on presentation

**Information Processing Limits**:
- **Cognitive Load**: Limited capacity to process information

**Description**:
- **Lower Returns**: Lower returns on Mondays
- **Weekend Effect**: Weekend information incorporation
- **Volatility**: Higher volatility on Mondays
- **Liquidity**: Lower liquidity on Mondays

**Behavioral Explanation**:
- **Sentiment Carryover**: Negative sentiment from weekend
- **Information Processing**: Delayed processing of weekend news
- **Mood Effects**: Monday mood effects on trading
- **Attention**: Reduced attention and focus

**Investment Implications**:
- **Timing**: Consider Monday trading patterns
- **Rebalancing**: Avoid Monday rebalancing
- **Options**: Monday option pricing effects
- **Risk Management**: Monday risk management
