# Module 1: Stock Market Foundations (Lessons 1-10)

## Overview
This foundational module covers the essential concepts of money, financial markets, and basic investment principles. Students will learn about the evolution of money, how financial markets work, and develop the fundamental mindset needed for successful investing.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the fundamental functions of money
- Learn about different types of financial markets
- Develop basic investment knowledge
- Build a strong financial mindset foundation

## Lesson Structure
1. **Lesson 1**: What is Money and How it Works
2. **Lesson 2**: Income vs. Expenses
3. **Lesson 3**: Saving vs. Investing
4. **Lesson 4**: Inflation Explained Simply
5. **Lesson 5**: Time Value of Money
6. **Lesson 6**: Compound Interest Basics
7. **Lesson 7**: Good Debt vs. Bad Debt
8. **Lesson 8**: Emergency Fund Concept
9. **Lesson 9**: Why Most People Stay Poor
10. **Lesson 10**: Wealth Mindset for Beginners

## Prerequisites
- None (This is the first module)

## Duration
- **Estimated Time**: 4-6 hours
- **Recommended Pace**: 1 lesson per day

## Learning Path Position
**Foundation Level - Module 1 of 16**

## Key Concepts Covered
- Money functions and evolution
- Personal finance fundamentals
- Basic investment principles
- Financial planning concepts
- Wealth-building mindset

## Assessment

- Reflection assignments
