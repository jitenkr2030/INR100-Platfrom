# Mutual Funds Deep Dive - Course Overview

## Course Information

**Course Title**: Mutual Funds Deep Dive
**Difficulty Level**: Intermediate
**Duration**: 3-4 hours
**Total Lessons**: 11
**XP Reward**: 200 XP + 18 XP per lesson
**Prerequisites**: Stock Market Foundations (Recommended)

## Course Description

This comprehensive intermediate course provides in-depth knowledge about mutual funds, covering everything from basic types to advanced strategies. Learn to become a sophisticated mutual fund investor who can select, evaluate, and manage professional fund investments effectively. Perfect for those ready to move beyond basic investing concepts.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:
- Differentiate between various types of mutual funds and their suitability
- Understand active vs. passive fund management approaches
- Calculate and interpret Net Asset Value (NAV) and expense ratios
- Evaluate fund performance using professional metrics
- Choose between direct and regular plans based on cost considerations
- Analyze fund manager track records and investment strategies
- Read and interpret fund factsheets and research reports
- Understand the significance of Assets Under Management (AUM)
- Implement Systematic Transfer Plans (STP) for optimal asset allocation
- Use Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWP) for regular income generation
- Build comprehensive mutual fund portfolios for different goals

## Course Structure

### Foundation Concepts (Lessons 1-4)

### Lesson 1: Types of Mutual Funds (Equity, Debt, Hybrid)
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Equity funds, debt funds, hybrid funds, sectoral funds, thematic funds
- **Learning Outcome**: Understanding of different fund categories and their characteristics

### Lesson 2: Index Funds vs. Active Funds
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Passive management, active management, tracking error, alpha generation
- **Learning Outcome**: Ability to choose between passive and active approaches

### Lesson 3: How NAV Works
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: NAV calculation, formula, impact on returns, pricing mechanics
- **Learning Outcome**: Understanding of fund pricing and valuation

### Lesson 4: What is Expense Ratio?
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Total expense ratio, management fees, administrative costs, impact on returns
- **Learning Outcome**: Understanding of fund costs and their long-term impact

### Fund Operations (Lessons 5-7)

### Lesson 5: How Mutual Funds Generate Returns
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Investment strategies, portfolio construction, risk management, return generation
- **Learning Outcome**: Knowledge of how professional fund management works

### Lesson 6: Direct Plan vs. Regular Plan
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Distribution costs, broker commissions, direct vs. regular pricing, cost comparison
- **Learning Outcome**: Ability to choose cost-effective investment options

### Lesson 7: What is a Fund Manager?
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Fund manager role, track record analysis, investment philosophy, team management
- **Learning Outcome**: Skills to evaluate fund management quality

### Analysis & Tools (Lessons 8-9)

### Lesson 8: How to Read a Factsheet
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Factsheet components, performance metrics, portfolio holdings, risk indicators
- **Learning Outcome**: Professional-level fund analysis skills

### Lesson 9: AUM and Why It Matters
- **Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Key Topics**: Assets Under Management, scale benefits, size vs. performance, fund maturity
- **Learning Outcome**: Understanding of fund scale and its implications

### Advanced Strategies (Lessons 10-11)

### Lesson 10: What is STP (Systematic Transfer Plan)?
- **Duration**: 25-30 minutes
- **Key Topics**: STP mechanics, asset allocation strategies, timing benefits, implementation
- **Learning Outcome**: Advanced portfolio management techniques

### Lesson 11: What is SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)?
- **Duration**: 25-30 minutes
- **Key Topics**: SWP mechanics, income planning, tax implications, retirement strategies
- **Learning Outcome**: Skills for regular income generation through funds
