**Psychological Foundation:**

**Market Psychology Applications:**

**Learning Outcomes:**

- Appreciate cultural and social influences on investment decisions
- Develop awareness of personal emotional investment patterns

### Lesson 2: Cognitive Biases in Investing (75 minutes)
**Major Bias Identification:**
- Confirmation bias: seeking supportive information only
- Overconfidence: overestimating investment abilities
- Anchoring: using irrelevant reference points
- Loss aversion: feeling losses more than equivalent gains
- Recency bias: over weighting recent events
- Availability heuristic: judging by easily recalled examples
- Herding behavior: following the crowd

**Real-World Applications:**
- Detailed examples from Indian market context
- Case studies of bias impact on portfolio performance
- Statistical evidence of bias costs
- Bias mitigation strategies and tools

**Practical Tools:**
- Bias recognition checklists
- Self-assessment questionnaires
- Decision audit frameworks
- Bias-proof investment processes

### Lesson 3: Emotions in Investing (80 minutes)
**Emotional Intelligence in Finance:**
- Fear: market corrections and loss anxiety
- Greed: unrealistic return expectations and risk-taking
- Hope: constructive vs destructive optimism
- Envy and social comparison effects
- Regret: learning from past decisions

**Market Emotional Cycles:**
- Euphoria phase: characteristics and investor behavior
- Panic phase: market crash psychology and recovery patterns
- Normalization phase: returning to rational behavior
- Cultural influences on emotional investing in India

**Emotional Management Strategies:**
