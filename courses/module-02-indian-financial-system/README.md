# Module 2: Indian Financial System
## Understanding India's Financial Infrastructure (Lessons 11-20)

### Course Overview
Welcome to Module 2! Now that you understand the basics of money, it's time to explore India's financial system. This module will demystify how money flows in our economy and introduce you to the key players who make the financial world work.

### Learning Objectives
By completing this module, you will:
- Understand the roles of RBI, SEBI, and other financial institutions
- Learn how the stock market operates in India
- Get comfortable with demat accounts and trading
- Grasp the complete money flow in financial markets
- Build confidence to start your investment journey

### Module Details
- **Total Lessons**: 10
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner to Intermediate
- **Prerequisites**: Module 1 (Money Basics)
- **XP Reward**: 2000 XP (200 XP per lesson)

### Course Structure

#### Lesson 11: Banks vs NBFCs
- Understanding traditional banks and their functions
- Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) explained
- Key differences and when to use each
- Digital banking revolution in India

#### Lesson 12: RBI Role Explained
- The Reserve Bank of India's central role
- Monetary policy and interest rate control
- Currency issuance and management
- Financial stability and supervision

#### Lesson 13: SEBI Role Explained (Education Only)
- Securities and Exchange Board of India's functions
- Investor protection and market regulation
- How SEBI protects retail investors
- Investor awareness and education

#### Lesson 14: Stock Market Overview (India)
- History and evolution of Indian stock markets
- Major indices: Nifty 50, Sensex, Bank Nifty
- Market participants and their roles
- Understanding market capitalization

#### Lesson 15: NSE vs BSE
- National Stock Exchange vs Bombay Stock Exchange
- Trading mechanisms and technology
- Market share and global recognition
- Which one should you choose for trading?

#### Lesson 16: Market Timings
- Trading hours and holiday calendar
- Pre-market and after-market sessions
- Different segment timings (equity, derivatives)
- International market influences

#### Lesson 17: What is a Demat Account
- Dematerialization revolution in India
- How demat accounts work
- Choosing the right depository participant
- Account opening process and documentation

#### Lesson 18: Trading Account Basics
- Difference between trading and demat accounts
- Order types and execution
- Market orders vs limit orders
- Understanding order book and trade confirmation

#### Lesson 19: Clearing & Settlement
- How trades are settled in India
- Role of clearing corporations
- T+2 settlement cycle
- Understanding settlement failures and penalties

#### Lesson 20: How Money Flows in Markets
- Complete money flow from investor to company
- Understanding primary and secondary markets
- How companies raise money from public
- Role of intermediaries and their earnings
