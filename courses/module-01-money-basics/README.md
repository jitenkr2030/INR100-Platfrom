# Module 1: Money Basics
## Complete Beginner Financial Education (Lessons 1-10)

### Course Overview
Welcome to the INR100 Learning Academy! This foundational module will transform your understanding of money, helping you build the essential financial literacy that every successful investor needs.

### Learning Objectives
By completing this module, you will:
- Understand what money is and how it works in our economy
- Learn the difference between saving and investing
- Master the concepts of inflation and compound interest
- Develop a wealth mindset for long-term financial success
- Build the foundation for all advanced financial concepts

### Module Details
- **Total Lessons**: 10
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Difficulty Level**: Beginner
- **Prerequisites**: None - this is your starting point!
- **XP Reward**: 2000 XP (200 XP per lesson)

### Course Structure

#### Lesson 1: What is Money and How it Works
- The evolution of money from barter to digital
- Money as a medium of exchange, store of value, and unit of account
- How money creation works in the modern economy
- Understanding currency and its role in your daily life

#### Lesson 2: Income vs Expenses
- Different types of income (salary, business, passive)
- Fixed vs variable expenses
- The importance of tracking your cash flow
- Income optimization strategies

#### Lesson 3: Saving vs Investing
- The difference between saving and investing
- When to save vs when to invest
- Building an emergency fund first
- The cost of not investing

#### Lesson 4: Inflation Explained Simply
- What inflation is and why it matters
- How inflation erodes purchasing power
- Historical inflation rates in India
- Protecting yourself from inflation

#### Lesson 5: Time Value of Money
- The concept that money today is worth more than money tomorrow
- Present value vs future value
- Discounting and compounding concepts
- Practical applications in daily life

#### Lesson 6: Compound Interest Basics
- Einstein's "eighth wonder of the world"
- How compound interest works over time
- The power of starting early
- Real-world examples and calculations

#### Lesson 7: Good Debt vs Bad Debt
- Understanding different types of debt
- When debt can be beneficial
- The danger of high-interest debt
- Building a debt strategy

#### Lesson 8: Emergency Fund Concept
- Why you need an emergency fund
- How much to save (3-6 months of expenses)
- Where to keep emergency funds
- Building your fund systematically

#### Lesson 9: Why Most People Stay Poor
- Common money mindset blocks
- The poverty cycle and how to break it
- Financial education gaps
- Changing your relationship with money

#### Lesson 10: Wealth Mindset for Beginners
- Developing abundance thinking
- Goal setting and visualization
- Creating positive money habits
- Building confidence for the journey ahead
