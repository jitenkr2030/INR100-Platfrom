# Module 13: Portfolio Management

## Overview
This comprehensive module covers advanced portfolio management techniques and strategies for building, maintaining, and optimizing investment portfolios. Students will learn about modern portfolio theory, asset allocation strategies, risk management, and emerging trends in portfolio management.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand modern portfolio theory and its applications
- Learn advanced asset allocation and portfolio construction techniques
- Master portfolio rebalancing and performance attribution methods
- Explore alternative investments and ESG considerations
- Understand the impact of technology on portfolio management
- Prepare for future trends in portfolio management

## Key Takeaways
- Portfolio management requires understanding both theory and practice
- Asset allocation is the primary driver of portfolio returns
- Regular rebalancing and performance monitoring are essential
- Alternative investments can enhance diversification
- ESG factors are becoming integral to portfolio management
- Technology is transforming portfolio management practices
- Continuous learning is necessary to stay current with trends
- Risk management must be integrated throughout the process
