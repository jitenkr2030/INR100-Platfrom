# Lesson 137: Digital Assets & Portfolio Management

## Understanding Digital Assets

Digital assets represent ownership or rights stored in digital format:
- **Cryptocurrencies**: Bitcoin, Ethereum, other digital currencies
- **Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs)**: Unique digital collectibles
- **Digital Securities**: Tokenized traditional securities
- **Utility Tokens**: Access to specific services or platforms
- **Stablecoins**: Cryptocurrency pegged to underlying assets

## Digital Asset Categories

### Cryptocurrencies
#### Major Cryptocurrencies
- **Bitcoin (BTC)**: Digital gold, store of value
- **Ethereum (ETH)**: Smart contract platform
- **Altcoins**: Alternative cryptocurrencies
- **Layer 2 Solutions**: Scaling solutions for blockchains

#### Cryptocurrency Characteristics
- **Decentralization**: No central authority control
- **Cryptography**: Secure cryptographic protocols
- **Blockchain Technology**: Distributed ledger system
- **Limited Supply**: Controlled monetary policy

### Tokenized Assets
#### Security Tokens
- **Equity Tokens**: Tokenized company shares
- **Debt Tokens**: Tokenized bonds or loans
- **Real Estate Tokens**: Tokenized property ownership
- **Commodity Tokens**: Tokenized physical commodities

#### Utility Tokens
- **Platform Tokens**: Access to specific services
- **Governance Tokens**: Voting rights in decentralized organizations
- **Reward Tokens**: Loyalty and reward programs
- **Access Tokens**: Exclusive access to content or services

## Digital Asset Risk Management

### Risk Categories
#### Market Risks
- **Price Volatility**: Significant price fluctuations
- **Market Manipulation**: Pump and dump schemes
- **Regulatory Changes**: Government regulation impact
- **Adoption Risk**: Slow mainstream adoption

#### Technology Risks
- **Smart Contract Bugs**: Code vulnerabilities
- **Network Security**: Blockchain network attacks
- **Private Key Loss**: Loss of access to digital assets
- **Exchange Hacks**: Centralized exchange security breaches

#### Operational Risks
- **Custody Risks**: Secure storage challenges
- **Regulatory Compliance**: KYC/AML requirements
- **Tax Implications**: Complex tax treatment
- **Reputation Risks**: Association with illegal activities

### Risk Mitigation Strategies
#### Technical Risk Management
#### Security Best Practices
- **Cold Storage**: Offline storage for long-term holdings
- **Multi-Signature**: Require multiple signatures for transactions
- **Hardware Wallets**: Dedicated hardware security
- **Backup Procedures**: Secure backup of private keys

#### Portfolio Risk Management
#### Position Sizing
- **Percentage Allocation**: Limit percentage in digital assets
- **Volatility-Based Sizing**: Adjust position size based on volatility
- **Stop-Loss Orders**: Automated selling at predetermined levels
- **Rebalancing**: Regular portfolio rebalancing
