## Detailed Curriculum Breakdown

### Lesson 1: Introduction to Risk Management (25 minutes)
**Risk Foundation:**
- Definition and importance of investment risk
- Types of risk: market, company-specific, inflation, liquidity
- Risk-return relationship and trade-offs
- Risk tolerance assessment and personal risk capacity

**Indian Market Context:**
- Regulatory environment (SEBI, RBI)
- Market structure risks and protection mechanisms
- Cultural factors affecting risk perception
- Banking system stability and deposit insurance

**Learning Outcomes:**
- Understand different types of investment risks
- Assess personal risk tolerance honestly
- Develop risk-aware investment mindset
- Recognize importance of systematic risk management

### Lesson 2: Understanding Volatility (35 minutes)
**Volatility Fundamentals:**
- Definition and measurement of price fluctuations
- Types of volatility: normal, event-driven, structural, seasonal
- Volatility across different asset classes
- Historical volatility patterns in Indian markets

**Measurement and Analysis:**
- Standard deviation calculation and interpretation
- Beta analysis for market sensitivity
- VIX (volatility index) understanding
- Maximum drawdown concepts

**Volatility Management:**
- Portfolio construction for different volatility levels
- SIP strategies for rupee cost averaging
- Market cycle positioning and tactical adjustments
- Emotional management during volatile periods

### Lesson 3: Risk Measurement Techniques (40 minutes)
**Quantitative Risk Metrics:**
- Standard deviation for return variability
- Beta for market sensitivity measurement
- Alpha for risk-adjusted performance
- Sharpe ratio for return per unit of risk

**Advanced Risk Measures:**
- Value at Risk (VaR) for maximum expected loss
- Maximum drawdown for peak-to-trough decline
- Conditional VaR for tail risk assessment
- Tracking error for benchmark deviation

**Practical Implementation:**
- Excel/Google Sheets calculation methods
- Online risk calculators and tools
- Portfolio risk dashboard creation
- Regular monitoring and alerting systems

### Lesson 4: Understanding Drawdowns (30 minutes)
**Drawdown Fundamentals:**
- Definition and calculation methods
- Types: minor (5-10%), moderate (10-20%), major (20-40%), severe (40%+)
- Historical drawdown analysis in Indian markets
- Recovery patterns and timeframes

**Psychological Impact:**
- Emotional responses to portfolio declines
- Behavioral biases during drawdowns
- Decision-making under stress
- Recovery strategies and patience requirements

**Management Strategies:**
- Prevention through diversification and quality focus
- During-drawdown systematic approaches
- Recovery optimization and opportunistic rebalancing
- Emergency fund utilization during crises

### Lesson 5: Diversification Strategies (45 minutes)
**Diversification Fundamentals:**
- "Don't put all eggs in one basket" principle
- Mathematical foundation and correlation benefits
- Asset class, geographic, sector, and style diversification
- Optimal diversification levels and over-diversification pitfalls

**Portfolio Construction:**
- Conservative, balanced, and growth portfolio allocations
- Indian market diversification strategies
- International diversification benefits and currency considerations
- Real-world portfolio examples and case studies

**Advanced Techniques:**
- Factor-based diversification (value, growth, quality)
- Dynamic asset allocation based on market conditions
- Sector rotation strategies for different economic cycles
- Rebalancing approaches and tax efficiency

### Lesson 6: Portfolio Protection Methods (35 minutes)
**Strategic Protection:**
- Position sizing and concentration limits
- Quality-based selection criteria
- Defensive asset allocation during volatility
- Insurance and hedging as portfolio protection

**Tactical Protection:**
- Stop-loss strategies and implementation
- Dynamic asset allocation adjustments
- Sector rotation for protection
- Cash management and liquidity planning

**Advanced Techniques:**
- Options-based protection strategies
- Correlation hedging and low-correlation assets
- Tail risk protection for extreme events
- Market condition-specific protection approaches

### Lesson 7: Emergency Planning & Exit Strategies (40 minutes)
**Emergency Fund Planning:**
- Calculation methods and adequacy assessment
- Liquidity tier management and optimization
- Emergency fund deployment procedures
- Crisis response planning and execution

**Exit Strategy Framework:**
- Individual investment exit criteria
- Portfolio-level exit strategies
- Performance-based and fundamental-based exits
- Systematic exit rules and implementation

**Crisis Response:**
- Market crisis response procedures
- Personal crisis management (job loss, medical emergency)
- Technology and infrastructure crisis preparation
- Professional help and family communication strategies
