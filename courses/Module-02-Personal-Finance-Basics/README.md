# Module 2: Personal Finance Basics (Lessons 11-20)

## Overview
This module builds upon the foundation of Module 1 by diving deeper into the Indian financial system, banking, and securities markets. Students will learn about banks, NBFCs, regulatory bodies, and how money flows in financial markets.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the Indian financial system structure
- Learn about banks, NBFCs, and their roles
- Understand RBI and SEBI functions
- Learn about stock markets and trading basics

## Lesson Structure
11. **Lesson 11**: Banks vs. NBFCs
12. **Lesson 12**: RBI Role Explained
13. **Lesson 13**: SEBI Role Explained
14. **Lesson 14**: Stock Market Overview India
15. **Lesson 15**: NSE vs. BSE
16. **Lesson 16**: Market Timings
17. **Lesson 17**: What is Demat Account
18. **Lesson 18**: Trading Account Basics
19. **Lesson 19**: Clearing Settlement
20. **Lesson 20**: How Money Flows in Markets

## Prerequisites
- Completion of Module 1: Stock Market Foundations

## Duration
- **Estimated Time**: 5-7 hours
- **Recommended Pace**: 1 lesson per day

## Learning Path Position
**Foundation Level - Module 2 of 16**

## Key Concepts Covered
- Indian banking system
- Regulatory framework (RBI, SEBI)
- Stock market mechanics
- Demat and trading accounts
- Money flow in markets

## Assessment

- Banking product comparison exercise
- Market mechanism understanding test
